Sara Tannous

Biochemist

Hello! My name is Sara Tannous, and I am currently pursuing my undergraduate degree in Biochemistry at the University of California, Riverside. My passion for science and discovery drives me to explore the intersection of chemistry and biology, where breakthroughs can make a tangible difference in the world around us. As a student, I enjoy the intellectual challenges that biochemistry offers and am deeply committed to using my skills to contribute to advancements in healthcare and medicine. Outside of academics, I actively contribute to my community through volunteering.

At the Riverside Free Clinic, I serve as a typist, working closely with pharmacists and healthcare professionals to provide accurate and efficient support for patients in need. This experience has not only strengthened my technical and organizational skills but has also deepened my understanding of the importance of accessible healthcare. Additionally, I volunteer at Loma Linda Hospital, where I assist in various tasks to help ensure that patients and their families feel supported during challenging times. This role has reinforced my belief in the importance of empathy and teamwork in healthcare settings. It has been a humbling experience to be part of such a dedicated team, working toward improving the well-being of others.

In my free time, I enjoy learning about new developments in science, exploring my interests in healthcare and innovation, and participating in activities that help me grow as a person and professional. My experiences have shaped me into a diligent and compassionate individual who is excited about the future of medicine and the role I can play in it. Thank you for visiting my website, and I hope to connect with you soon!
